What you are about to see

A human keeps their Source Vault sealed and gives an AI agent a limited Spend Cell for one job. The agent can spend inside that cell, but cannot touch the original money source.

Why this matters

This is the missing layer between ordinary payment APIs and autonomous agents: spend authority that is minted, bounded, explainable, and independently verifiable.

Before Unsafe money exposure

Builders usually hand an agent a payment key, card, or one-off approval hack.

During Spend Cell minted

The runtime creates a Spend Mandate, Spend Cell, spend key, and Spend Intent for this one job.

After Spend Trace attached

The payment result includes portable evidence that another system can verify.

Technical evidence

These artifacts are what a developer, auditor, counterparty, or verifier would inspect. Customers should understand the story above first; the JSON proves the story is real.
