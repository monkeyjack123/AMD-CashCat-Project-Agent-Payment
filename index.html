How CashCat works.

CashCat starts with digital spend like APIs, data, software, subscriptions, and cloud resources, but the system is built for any governed payment an AI agent is allowed to make.

1 User gives a task

The experience starts with a business objective, workflow, or operating task, not a payment form.

2 AI proposes a purchase

The model returns which paid resources are needed, why they matter, and what they should cost.

3 CashCat applies controls

Budget, category, approval, and authority rules decide whether the spend can proceed.

4 Proof and receipt are returned

The outcome is inspectable, auditable, and machine-verifiable for finance and ops teams.

The AI decides. CashCat governs.
